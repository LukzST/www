FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Got Questions?

Everything you need to know about LIGHT. Find answers to the most common questions below.

What is LIGHT?

▼

LIGHT is an atmospheric horror game where you play as Operator 07, the last sentinel in an isolated relay bunker. The "Fade" has consumed the stars, and you must keep the generator running while uncovering corporate secrets and deciding the fate of a fading reality.

What are the system requirements?

▼

Minimum: A toaster (2015 model), Windows 10, 1GB RAM, any dual-core CPU.
Recommended: A smart toaster (with WiFi), Windows 11, 2GB RAM, any quad-core CPU.
Note: Game may toast your bread while you play. Not responsible for burnt toast.

Is LIGHT free?

▼

Yes! LIGHT is completely free and open-source. You can download it from Itch.io or GitHub. No payment required, no hidden costs.

Is there a demo available?

▼

Yes! You can play the LIGHT demo right now. Just click on the "Demo" button in the sidebar or visit our Demo page to download and experience the atmospheric horror before the full release.

What platforms is LIGHT available on?

▼

Currently, LIGHT is available for Windows (64-bit). Linux and Mac versions are planned for future updates.

How do I report a bug?

▼

You can report bugs on our GitHub Issues page. Please include as much detail as possible (screenshots, steps to reproduce, system specs).

Where can I listen to the soundtrack?

▼

The complete LIGHT soundtrack is available on our Soundtrack page. You can listen to all tracks directly on YouTube.

Can I contribute to development?

▼

Absolutely! LIGHT is open-source. You can contribute code, report issues, or suggest features on GitHub. We welcome all contributions!

How can I contact the team?

▼

You can reach us via email at contatosadberry@gmail.com or through our social media channels (GitHub, Instagram, YouTube).

How often is LIGHT updated?

▼

We release updates regularly. Check our DevLogs for development progress, patch notes, and upcoming features.
